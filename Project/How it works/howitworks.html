How it Works

With us, getting your customized logo design is a piece of cake

As a Logo Design Firm, we realize the importance of logo in your business. We can deliver your custom logo design in one day. We also offer as many revisions as you like. We guarantee that our work will make your company stand out from the rest of the crowd and make a long lasting impression on your clients.

You Fill Out the Order Form.

Once you choose the package for your corporate logo design you want to order, fill out our simple and secure order form with the information about your company and design specifications for your logo. Confirm the order by paying the amount due.

We Assign Designers to Your Project...

We will assign designers to your logo design project according to the package you choose and your relevant industry. Work on your logo concepts will start immediately.

You Receive the Logo Samples...

You'll receive logo samples at your account area and your account manager will explain you the details of the concept. You can review and select the one you like, if needed, you can ask for revision

You Receive the Final Logo in Different File Formats...

Once you make the final choice, you will receive all the different file formats and logo ownership. Remember, you will still be entitled to our after-sales support (for life!) and can contact us for any further assistance.
